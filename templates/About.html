About SehatAI

What is SehatAI?

SehatAI is an AI-powered health assistance system designed to analyze user-described symptoms and provide informational health insights. It helps users understand possible health conditions and recognize when medical attention may be required.

SehatAI does not replace doctors, clinics, or hospitals. It exists to reduce uncertainty and guide users toward informed decisions.

How SehatAI Works

  • Symptom Extraction: User input is processed using keyword detection and symptom alias mapping.
  • Safety Checks: High-risk symptoms such as bleeding, severe chest pain, or breathing difficulty immediately trigger medical warnings.
  • Machine Learning: A trained Naive Bayes model analyzes symptom patterns and estimates probable conditions.
  • Context Awareness: Food-related illness, mild viral cases, and low-confidence inputs are handled separately to avoid misleading output.

Medical Disclaimer

SehatAI provides informational suggestions only and is not a medical diagnosis system. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for diagnosis, treatment, or emergencies.
